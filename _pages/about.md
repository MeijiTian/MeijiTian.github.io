I am Xuanyu Tian (田烜宇 in Chinese), a final year Ph.D. student at [ShanghaiTech University](https://www.shanghaitech.edu.cn) (Shanghai, China), advised by Prof. [Yuyao Zhang](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=gIE0JTAAAAAJ&hl). Before that, I received my B.E. degree of Computer Science and Technology from Wuhan University of Technology in 2021.

I am dedicated to **solving medical inverse problems** within a scarce data and low SNR scenarios. Currently, my research focuses on:
* Dynamic MRI Reconstruction <br>
* MRI Motion Correction  <br>
* Optimization Framework <br>
* Self-supervised Image Restoration <br>

See more in the [detailed CV](https://MeijiTian.github.io/assets/pdf/CV_XuanyuTian.pdf)
