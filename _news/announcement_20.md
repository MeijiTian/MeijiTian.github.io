Three papers on inverse problem solving were accepted to ICML 2026 :tada:

- [PnP-ADMM + Diffusion](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.23214) improves robustness in medical inverse problem solving.
- [CaMB-Diff](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.01890) models structured forward operators for unknown dynamic range compression tasks, including low-field MRI, low-light and HDR restoration.
- IPOD introduces a reference-free meta-learning strategy for INR-based MRI reconstruction.
